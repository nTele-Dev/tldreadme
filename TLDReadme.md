  intelligence out. 

  That's the whole thesis of TLDREADME in one sentence: make the context so good that the LLM can't help but be smart about your code.


~~~

+After running `init`, TLDREADME generates `.claude/TLDR.md` in the target directory. 

This is the "next-token" feature — it serializes TLDREADME's knowledge into a file that any LLM can consume on startup. Auto-updated by the watcher.
